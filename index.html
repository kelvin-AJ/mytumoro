The Tumoro ELO System

A Dynamic, Fair, and Reliable Measure of Talent

This system is designed to move beyond static resumes, offering a continuously updated rating that reflects true skill and credibility. It's built on a dual-ELO foundation: one for Talents and one for the Reviewers who vet them, ensuring quality and accuracy at every step.

The Talent ELO Journey

Every journey starts somewhere. New talents begin with a provisional ELO rating of:

1200

This rating evolves rapidly based on initial performance, guided by a variable K-Factor that stabilizes as more data is collected.

Rating Adjustment Speed (K-Factor)

The K-Factor determines how much a Talent's ELO changes after each interaction. The system adapts this factor based on the Talent's experience on the platform to ensure both agility for new users and stability for established ones.

What Fuels the ELO Engine?

A Talent's ELO isn't just a number; it's a composite score derived from multiple, weighted interactions. This multi-faceted approach ensures a holistic and fair representation of a candidate's abilities and desirability.

ELO Modifier Impact

Different interactions carry different weights. High-stakes events like interviews and skill reviews have a greater impact than passive signals of interest, ensuring the ELO accurately reflects proven ability.

The Guardian of Quality: Reviewer ELO

A rating system is only as reliable as its inputs. To ensure the integrity of Talent reviews, every verified Reviewer also has their own ELO. This internal rating measures their accuracy and consistency, giving more weight to feedback from more trusted sources.

Factors Defining a Reviewer's Reliability

A Reviewer's ELO isn't arbitrary. It's calculated based on how their assessments correlate with real-world outcomes and peer consensus, ensuring a self-correcting system of accountability.

Agreement with Peers

Do their ratings align with other high-ELO reviewers assessing the same talent?

Correlation with Outcomes

Do talents they rate highly succeed in interviews and get hired?

Feedback from Talents

Is their feedback consistently rated as fair and helpful by the talents they review?

Moderation History

Are their reviews consistently high-quality, or do they require admin intervention?

Ensuring Fairness & Mitigating Bias

A fair system requires robust safeguards. We've built in multiple mechanisms to prevent manipulation and ensure that every talent's ELO is a true reflection of their merit.

🛡️

Dispute Mechanism

Talents can challenge unfair reviews, triggering an admin moderation process.

⚖️

Weighted Contributions

Reviews from higher-ELO reviewers have a greater impact, rewarding reliability.

📈

System Monitoring

Admins monitor for unusual ELO patterns or signs of collusion to maintain system integrity.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Multiple Data Points

ELO is aggregated from many sources, smoothing out the impact of any single biased opinion.
